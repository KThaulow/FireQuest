What is FIRE?

FIRE stands for Financial Independence, Retire Early. It's a movement dedicated to helping you achieve financial freedom by saving aggressively, investing wisely, and living below your means. The goal is to reach a point where you have enough savings and investments to retire far earlier than the traditional retirement age.
